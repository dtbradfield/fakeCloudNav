About Us

Cloud Navigators delivers intelligent and cost efficient solutions to our clients through the power of the cloud. By harnessing the cloud, we strive to assist organizations in transforming their future with a dual strategy. First by navigating clients from their current on-premises messaging solution to the cloud and then by increasing their efficiency with Microsoft’s Office 365 collaboration suite. This transformation results in the client lowering their infrastructure costs as well as getting control of their email risk through additional cloud based solutions built around the client’s unique requirements for identity, security and compliance.


Cloud Navigators offers a range of services with a powerful set of tools and methodologies with a proven track record. Our consulting expertise with decades of combined experience allows us to assess organizational needs and then migrate them to and manage them on the Microsoft cloud. Our customers benefit from a remarkable ROI. We pride ourselves on high performance implementation delivery projects, steadfast managed services, and being a ‘trusted partner’. Cloud Navigators will flexibly adapt with your organization whatever tomorrow looks like.
