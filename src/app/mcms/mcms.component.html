Migrate to Microsoft Office 365 as a Turn-Key Hosted Service with Managed Cloud Migration Services

Are you looking to move to Microsoft’s Office 365? Managed Cloud Migration Services (MCMS) from Cloud Navigators is one of the easiest ways to migrate to Office 365. With minimal customer involvement needed, as well as zero user downtime, detailed progress and migration reports, it’s the most cost effective way to migrate email, contacts, calendars and tasks to Microsoft Office 365.


No On-Site Consultants or Hardware Needed

Managed Cloud Migration Services eliminates the need for high cost on-site consultants by offering secure remote services from our experienced engineers. In addition, no hardware investment is needed either as our process leverages Microsoft’s Azure Cloud to host the entire migration process.


No Expertise on Migration Needed

You and your team don’t need to learn any migration procedures or software. Our engineers, who have helped migrate hundreds of customers around the world, perform every step of our proven best practices methodology from initial briefing to the launch of your new messaging environment in Office 365.


Migrate Email, Calendar, Contacts and To-Do’s

Managed Cloud Migration Services migrates all current email, calendar entries, future calendar entries, contacts and to-do entries. There are no limitations; we will work with you to set parameters for how much data you migrate for your users of their email, calendar entries, contacts, and to-do entries.


The IAM Cloud Advantage

Another advantage is if you decide to use IAM Cloud, an Azure-based service for identity and access management (which eliminates the need for on-premises ADFS or DirSync), we will leverage IAM Cloud to provision users for the migration. In addition, IAM Cloud provides the following ongoing benefits: Single Sign-on from any directory, or even through your web portal, to any standards based application; business continuity for SaaS, unlike other solutions if your directory is unavailable for whatever reason, people are still able to authenticate to their online apps from any location with internet access; password management; Office 365 license management; cloud-hosted identity store for CSPs and OEMs; true SAML 2.0 Federation, and alumni identity hosting.
